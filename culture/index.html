Water as a cultural force.

Water is more than a resource: it is part of our culture, identity, and creativity. We support artistic interventions, theater, concerts, and public participation that bring people on the water and raise awareness.

Programs & formats

  • Theater: Novecento, Transitopia, ASSEDO – Odessa’s Journey
  • Circus: Sea Clown Sailing Circus, aerial missions on Hanseat
  • Concerts: RioKlangBerlin and more
  • Podium discussions on river swimming and access
